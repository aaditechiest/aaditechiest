How to Pick the Best Kettle for You

Consider your primary needs. If you need a larger capacity, the Kitchoff or Pigeon models are great. For specific functions like an egg boiler, almost all models offer it. Pay attention to the material; stainless steel is durable, while glass allows you to see the contents. For specific brand preference, AGARO and Pigeon are well-known brands.
